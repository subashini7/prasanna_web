### 3D position localization in detector-limited systems

3D point-spread functions can be shaped to
increase accuracies over a 3D volume. In the
detector-limited regime, the gain in accuracy occurs in all three
dimensions throughout the axial range of interest. The PSF is shaped as
a double-helix, resulting in a system with fundamentally better 3D
localization-accuracies than standard-PSF systems, capable of achieving
single-image sub-nanometer accuracies.
