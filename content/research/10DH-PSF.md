### Double Helix Point Spread Function

The Double-helix point spread function is an engineered PSF specifically designed for 3D imaging. The PSF design involves an optimization procedure that applies constraints in the Gauss-Laguerre modal plane, the spatial domain, and the Fourier domain, and achieves over thirty times improvement in transfer function efficiency.  [See [Opt. Exp. 16, 3484 (2008)](http://www.opticsexpress.org/abstract.cfm?id=154737) ]
