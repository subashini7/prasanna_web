Oh Hello! 👋 My name is... actually, my first name has 3 words. But you can call me Prasanna. I've built products at Dishcare, Osmo, Orbital, Exnodes, Arecont, Ricoh, KLA, Omnivision & D. E. Shaw.

I was a Postdoc at Caltech & earned a PhD from the University of Colorado Boulder and a BE from GCT Coimbatore. I then studied Business at Stanford University & was an editor of Optica before serving as a panel member of the U.S. National Science Foundation. My 31 patents & 45 papers have been cited by 2360 papers with ingenious ideas I wish I had thought of.

My work has won the University of Colorado outstanding PhD award, U.S. EB-1 extraordinary ability classification, Optica outstanding paper award, SPIE science & engineering award, CPIA awards & the GCT Coimbatore gold medal.
