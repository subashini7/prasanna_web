I've built products for consumers & enterprises at Dishcare, Osmo, Orbital, Exnodes, Arecont, Ricoh, KLA, Omnivision & D. E. Shaw. Some say consumers are harder to please than enterprises. I think they need to meet my enterprise customers.

I was a Postdoc at Caltech & earned a PhD from the University of Colorado Boulder and a BE from GCT Coimbatore. I studied Business at Stanford University & was an editor of Optica before serving as a panel member of the U.S. National Science Foundation. My 31 patents & 45 papers have been cited by 2360 ingenious papers with ideas I can't believe I didn't think of.

My work has won the University of Colorado outstanding PhD award, U.S. EB-1 extraordinary ability classification, Optica outstanding paper award, SPIE science & engineering award, CPIA awards & the GCT Coimbatore gold medal. Some say gold medals are actually made out of silver. Aren't they missing the point?
