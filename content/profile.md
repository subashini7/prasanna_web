Across experiences spanning the CEO of Dishcare, Head of Product Infra at Osmo, Head of Content Infra at Orbital, CEO of Exnodes, VP Engineering at Arecont and senior roles at Ricoh Innovations, KLA and D. E. Shaw, Prasanna has been fortunate to build products in a range of industries.

Prasanna was a Postdoc at Caltech and holds a PhD from the University of Colorado Boulder and a BE from GCT Coimbatore. He studied Business at Stanford University and served as an Editor of Optica before serving as a panel member of the U.S. National Science Foundation. Prasanna's 31 patents and 45 publications have earned 2345 citations.

Prasanna's work has been recognized by the University of Colorado Outstanding PhD Award, U.S. EB-1 Extraordinary Ability Classification, Optica Outstanding Paper Award, SPIE Science & Engineering Award, CPIA awards and the GCT Coimbatore Gold Medal.
