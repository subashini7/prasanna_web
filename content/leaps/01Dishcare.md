### AI robot to knock our most hated chore

[Dishcare](https://dish.care) is a dishwasher that loads, cleans and puts away dishes instantly. So you can save time and help our planet while enjoying real dishes. Powered by a Dish AI to comprehend collections of dishes and a rolling grasp robot to handle a variety of dish types, dishcare cleans instantly so dirty dishes don’t wait around and grow germs. It targets dirt in seconds, so you can enjoy ultra clean dishes while saving energy and water. Dishcare has the size of a standard 24" dishwasher for an easy upgrade. Dishcare licenses technology to leaders in consumer appliances, industrial automation and more.
