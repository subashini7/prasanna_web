### Spatio temporal low light architecture

Security video at low light levels is plagued by darkness, noise and motion blur. STELLAR enables streaming color video at 0.01 lux, about 100x dimmer than a moonlit night. By improving video quality, while simultaneously reducing bitrate and storage requirements, STELLAR redefines possibilities in low light imaging.
