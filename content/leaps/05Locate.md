### 3D position localization

3D PSFs can be shaped to increase accuracies over a 3D volume. In the detector limited regime, the gain in accuracy occurs in 3D throughout the axial range of interest. The PSF is shaped as a double helix, resulting in a system with fundamentally better 3D localization accuracies than standard PSF systems, capable of achieving single image sub nanometer accuracies.
[APL (2009)](http://link.aip.org/link/?APPLAB/95/021103/1), [SPIE (2009)](https://www.spiedigitallibrary.org/conference-proceedings-of-spie/7184/71840I/3D-microscopy-with-a-double-helix-point-spread-function/10.1117/12.816585.short), [Video](http://ftp.aip.org/epaps/appl_phys_lett/E-APPLAB-95-078925/078925apl1_mm.mov).
