### 3D imager design through multi view optimization
      
Standard 3D imagers exhibit a tradeoff between device size and 
position accuracy. We design compact and accurate multi view imagers by optimizing 
subsystem parameters using a multi view simulator. We find high accuracy 
multi view configurations that minimize a quality metric based on position uncertainties estimated using a closed form analytical solution and a Monte Carlo simulation. [COSI 11](http://www.opticsinfobase.org/abstract.cfm?URI=COSI-2011-JTuD4), [US9245445B2](https://patents.google.com/patent/US8542268B2), [COSI 13](http://www.opticsinfobase.org/abstract.cfm?URI=ISA-2013-JTu4A.21), [US9851475B2](https://patents.google.com/patent/US9851475B2).
