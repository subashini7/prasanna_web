### 0.01 lux video streaming

Video at low light levels is plagued by darkness, noise and motion blur. Here we improve video quality in such challenging conditions with a spatio temporal low light architecture (STELLAR). STELLAR enables streaming color video at 0.01 lux (100x less than a moon lit night). By improving video quality, while simultaneously reducing bitrate and storage requirements, STELLAR redefines possibilities in low light imaging.
