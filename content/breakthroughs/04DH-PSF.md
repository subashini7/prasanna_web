### Double helix point spread function

The double helix point spread function is an engineered PSF specifically designed for 3D imaging. The PSF design involves an optimization procedure that applies constraints in the Gauss-Laguerre modal plane, the spatial domain, and the Fourier domain, and achieves over thirty times improvement in transfer function efficiency.  [Opt. Exp. 16, 3484 (2008)](http://www.opticsexpress.org/abstract.cfm?id=154737), [Double Helix PSF Video](http://www.opticsinfobase.org/oe/viewmedia.cfm?uri=oe-16-5-3484&seq=3).
