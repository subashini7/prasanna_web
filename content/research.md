Computational systems that combine Computational Optics, Imaging, Sensing, and Parallel Processing have the ability to capture information that is untapped in classical systems. In the last few years, we have had the opportunity to apply this computational sensing paradigm to semiconductor fabrication, nanoscopy, microscopy, surveillance, 3D imaging, phase imaging, opto-fluidics, chip-scale imaging, and energy harnessing.
